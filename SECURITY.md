<img src="Assets/entelgia-logo.png" alt="Entelgia Logo" width="120"/> # Security Policy

Entelgia is an experimental, research-oriented AI system.
Security is approached as a design concern, not a claim of complete protection.

---

## Scope

This project explores persistent memory, internal dialogue, and self-regulation
in AI systems. As such:

- Unexpected behaviors may occur
- Meta-instructions or internal artifacts may surface
- The system should not be used in safety-critical or adversarial environments

---

## Data & Privacy Notes

- Entelgia stores memory locally on the user's machine
- No data is transmitted externally except to the configured local LLM runtime
- Memory can be manually deleted by removing local data files (see README)

Users are responsible for reviewing and managing their local data.

---

## Experimental Nature

This project is under active research and development.
Security mechanisms may change as the architecture evolves.

There is no guarantee of protection against all misuse or failure modes.
