Code of Conduct
Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members. We pledge to treat everyone with respect, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, political belief, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

Using welcoming and inclusive language
Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
Focusing on what is best for the community
Showing empathy towards other community members
Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

Harassment or discrimination of any kind
Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
Trolling or insulting comments
Any form of aggression or intimidation
Enforcement
Community members who violate this code of conduct may be:

Asked to stop the behavior
Temporarily banned from the project
Permanently removed from the project
To report violations, contact: sivanhavkin@github.com
